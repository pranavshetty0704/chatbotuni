VCET Alumni Association
An alumni meet is organized regularly. Alumni?s are contacted through various social media like
face book, WhatsApp etc. and also invited through personal emails. The date of alumni meet is
floated on institute website the alumni?s are appealed to register online for the same. The meet
comprises of a welcome program, Interaction, entertainment programs like singing, dancing followed

interaction improves industry contacts for placement, project and internship. The institute has a

etc. to share their experience about the latest trend in industry. It helps the current students to getScraped Data

serve as external project examiners for university examination.
